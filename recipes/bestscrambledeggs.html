Best Scrambled Eggs

These scrambled eggs are the best!

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup half-n-half
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 TBSP unsalted butter
  • black pepper

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, half-n-half, and salt.
  2. Melt the bitter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the eggs to the center of the pan and reduce the heat to medium-low.
  4. Gently push the eggs from one end of the pan to the other. Remove eggs from heat while there is still some moisture showing.
  5. Transfer to serving plates. Sprinkle with pepper.
