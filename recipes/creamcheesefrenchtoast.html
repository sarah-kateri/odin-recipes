Cream Cheese French Toast

This french toast has cream cheese in it!

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 slices of bread
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 TBSP vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Filling:

  • 8 oz whipped cream cheese, softened
  • 2 TBSP powdered sugar
  • dash of ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat together cream cheese, powdered sugar, cinnamon and vanilla until fluffy.
  2. Spread a slice of bread with cream cheese filling. Top with another piece of bread to make a sandwich.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, vanilla and ground cinnamon. Dip entire sandwich into egg mixture.
  4. Over medium heat, melt butter in a pan.
  5. Add coated sandwich to the skillet. Cook on both sides evenly until they are browned. Repeat with remaining bread and filling.
  6. Serve with syrup and butter.
