Dutch Baby Pancake

This is a big pancake!

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 TBSP sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • powdered sugar, maple syrup, or jam for serving

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, blend the flour, milk, eggs, sugar, vanilla, and salt.
  2. Rest the batter for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Place a 9-10 inch cast iron pan on the middle rack of the oven. Heat the oven to 425 degrees.
  4. Melt the butter in the hot pan.
  5. Pour the batter on top of the butter. Place the skillet in the oven.
  6. Bake 15 to 20 minutes until lightly brown.
  7. Dust with powdered sugar. Cut into wedges and serve with maple syrup or jam.
  8. For fruit-filled Dutch baby: Arrange blueberries, raspberries, or sliced peaches over the bottom of the pan and pour batter on top.
