Maca Sausage Muffins

These muffins are made out of meat!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs ground pork
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 TBSP maca powder
  • 1 TBSP cinnamon
  • 1 TBSP nutmeg
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add mixture to muffin tins.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Let cool for 10 minutes.
